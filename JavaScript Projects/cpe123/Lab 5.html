Parametric Equation:

X = (A * sin(t) * cos(-C * t)) - (B * cos(t) * sin(-C * t)
Y = (A * sin(t) * sin(-C * t)) + (B * cos(t) * cos(-C * t)

A - outer radius

B - inner radius (B can be greater than A or negative, or both)

C - inner designs of circle

Speed of drawing (0 < speed < 20)
